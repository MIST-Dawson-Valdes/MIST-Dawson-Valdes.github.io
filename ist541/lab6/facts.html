My Pet Rock!

Facts

  • Common Name: Pet Rock
  • Average Size: 2–5 inches in diameter
  • Weight: 200–500 grams
  • Lifespan: Indefinite (eternal, barring erosion or extreme circumstances)
Anatomy
  1. Calm Core – The emotional center that keeps the rock perpetually relaxed.
  2. Obedient Structure – Ensures the rock always stays where you put it (no wandering off).
  3. Sedentary Surface – A smooth or rough exterior designed for maximum inactivity.
  4. Zero-Maintenance Coat – No grooming required; dust and dirt are considered fashionable accessories.
  5. Loyal Layer – Always by your side, but won’t demand walks or belly rubs.
  6. Unblinking Eyes – Though invisible, these never get tired of staring into the void of eternity.
  7. Weighty Personality – Subtle, yet undeniably present in every conversation.
  8. Smooth Communication Skills – Silent, but speaks volumes through its steadfast nature.
  9. Invisible Heart of Stone – Unyielding, metaphorically and literally.
